OUR PEOPLE

  • Amara G. Chima-Azubuike

    (Founder & CEO)

    Our founder is an alumni of the University of Port Harcourt, Rivers State where she studied Human Anatomy. Her love for creating things in the food space spurred her thoughts, dream and vision for healthy eating and living. She spent her one year of National Youth Service with the Society for Family Health (SFH) where she directly collaborated with other teams as peer educator for training and product marketing with focus on youths and upwardly mobile individuals.

    Amara is very analytical, detailed, tech savvy and communicates effectively; with deep knowledge of cause-effect unbundling. In addition to her position as the founder/CEO, she is hands-on as the production and quality control/assurance manager. Her tasks include the day to day running of the company’s operations and developing new lines of products. She also oversees the eco-sustainability project of the company. As a serial entrepreneur, she also sits on the board of ZoeOptions Nigeria Limited (a player in the Oil & Gas sector). She is also the proprietor of Graceprince Nigeria Enterprises (a logistics and general trading outfit). Her dream is to dominate the innovative/healthy continental food space beginning from Nigeria.

    In furtherance of her child care/education volunteer activities, she is currently enrolled with Modern Montessori International, London; with a DMT certification in view. The company is well anchored under her watch.

  • Ihechimere Elijah Akomas

    (Sales Manager)

    Elijah is a graduate of Accountancy from the Abia State Polytechnic, Aba. He worked briefly with the Living Word Church Aba as an operations and safety manager before relocating to Port Harcourt to join CreekBay Farms. He trained as a piggery expert and has thereafter managed the piggery business of the company for over 3 years. Elijah is a very energetic, passionate and go to personality. His wealth of experience and ability to get along with people will help drive the sales and marketing aspect of the company’s business.

  • Goddy I. Alim

    (Finance/Accounts Manager)

    Goddy holds an HND in Banking & Finance from the Institute of Management & Technology (IMT), Enugu. He is also a chartered banker by training. He worked briefly as an operations officer with Zenith Bank Plc and a marketing executive with Stanbic IBTC Bank. He was a supervisor with G.O.A Nigeria Limited and a onetime senior administrative staff of Gastech Energy Solutions Limited.

    Goody is versed in production economics and book keeping. He is very un-assuming but an astute entrepreneur. He will bring his wealth of experience to bear on the development of CreekBay Farms to a world-class status.
