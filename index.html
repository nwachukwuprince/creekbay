About CreekBay

CreekBay Farms (CreekBay) was birthed by a divinely inspired vision. The brand is a vehicle for delivering a unique perspective on healthy eating and living to the African continent and beyond. Her flagship product – red rice flour was accidentally discovered in early 2020 through a series of home food trials by the founder and her children. They were only on a mission to trial healthy food options during the global lockdown. Since then, they have gone ahead to conduct several other theoretical studies and examinations to back their experiences. One major experience among others was the phenomenal all person’s healthy weight loss without exercise whilst presenting a glowing skin just by merely consuming red rice. Red rice flour has also been trialed in baking and confectionaries with resounding success

It is that physiological evidence that has been innovated around and upon; commercialized to benefit others. So far, the testimonials have been awesome. The company is given to constant and consistent evaluation of food options in our continent with a view to presenting them to the pubic in a more nutritious and healthy form. During the company’s food revolution, she sure would be introducing a chain of healthy, safe and affordable alternatives to existing everyday food and snacks.

CreekBay is headquartered in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. It currently operates from its state-of-the-art factory (approved by the Nigeria’s National Agency for Food & Drug Administration – NAFDAC). She is committed to continuous innovation and tinkering of the food space in a bid to be different. The company’s journey shall be rested on her vision, mission and values.

Vision

“to instill in people of all ages across our continent, the consciousness of living a quality life through healthy eating whilst creating values for sustainability”.

Mission

“We shall continually lead an out-to-in innovative approach at crafting and sustaining a brand which shall be synonymous with quality, safe, healthy and cost-friendly food products; leaving indelible nutritional and physical experiences with the consumers. We shall always strive to preserve our values by doing the right things.

Values:

integrity, innovation, excellence, concern for people, customer-centrism and health consciousness.
